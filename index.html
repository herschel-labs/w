Hello

...and welcome! My name is Robert and I am freelance based web designer/developer.

I am combining web programming with the artistic element...

Creating modern web experience using cutting-edge technologies. I was passionate about art since I can remember, and as an IT student, I am naturally inclined to creative problem solving. My personal relationship with what I do is of utmost importance to me and also reflects my performance. That stands behind my decision to act as a "company of one", combining two of my biggest niches...

When I am not busy building web solutions for companies, products, or events, I like to reach for one of my guitars and play or compose some rock or jazzy tunes. I also like devouring books in all forms - anything from science, philosophy or personal development shelf will go. And as a huge fan of informal education, I am spending an unhealthy portion of my free time learning new things across various fields, using resources like Udemy.
